About Hornet Haven

Hornet Haven is a custom modded Rust server built for players who want more than vanilla. With plenty of custom plugins, quality-of-life improvements and regular events, it delivers a smoother, more exciting Rust experience while keeping the core survival feel alive. Built for builders, grinders and anyone after a relaxed Rust home.
