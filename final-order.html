About Final Order

Final Order is the Rust home of GoldStandardX Gaming, built for players who want true freedom of playstyle. Whether you grind, build or fight, the server is balanced for every kind of player. Our mission is simple: a high-quality Rust experience with active staff, consistent wipes and a community that keeps growing.

Rules & Events

Welcome to GoldStandardX Gaming Community. This is our PVE Rust server.

1️⃣ General Conduct

  • Treat all players with respect.
  • No harassment, racism, sexism, hate speech, trolling or toxicity.
  • No impersonating admins, moderators or staff.
  • English only in global chat (unless specified otherwise). Use Google Translate if needed.
  • Keep global chat clear of spam and excessive caps.

2️⃣ PvE Rules (No Player Killing)

  • No KOS (Kill On Sight).
  • No raiding, damaging or griefing player bases.
  • No stealing from unlocked bases or tool cupboards.
  • Do not intentionally push or bait players into PvP zones.

3️⃣ Bases & Building

  • Do not block monuments, roads, rivers, quarries or recyclers.
  • No building within designated monument block zones.
  • Do not wall off entire areas or resources.
  • Keep tool cupboards secured.
  • Large clan compounds must not block access to major areas.

4️⃣ Raiding & Damage

  • Raiding and damaging is strictly prohibited.
  • No griefing by placing walls, doors or objects on other players' bases.
  • Do not exploit decay mechanics to take over bases.

5️⃣ PvP Zones

  • PvP is only allowed in marked PvP areas or during server events.
  • No camping safe zone exits.
  • No excessive spawn killing in PvP zones.
  • Looting is allowed only inside active PvP zones.

6️⃣ Events

  • Follow all admin instructions during events.
  • Event loot is only valid during the active event.
  • No exploiting event mechanics.

7️⃣ Exploits & Cheating

  • No hacking, scripting or third-party cheats.
  • No exploiting bugs, glitches or map errors.
  • Report bugs to admins immediately.
  • Using exploits may result in a wipe of your base and inventory.

8️⃣ Admin & Staff

  • Admin decisions are final.
  • Do not argue in global chat — open a support ticket if needed.
  • Do not spam admins for free items or teleportation.
  • Abuse of support systems will result in penalties.

9️⃣ Vehicles & Loot

  • Do not hoard server vehicles excessively.
  • Respect community loot spawns.
  • No blocking roads with vehicles.

😀 Wipes & Server Restarts

  • Map wipes occur monthly.
  • Restart schedule: Mon–Fri 7:00 AM & 9 PM CST; Sat & Sun when the server is free.
  • The server is not responsible for lost items due to crashes, but admins may reimburse at their discretion.

🚨 Rule Enforcement

Violations may result in: warning · item removal · sent to server jail · base removal · temporary ban · permanent ban.

🔥 420 Drugged & Boosted – Rust PVE Events

🚁 Helicopter Hunt

PvE Combat Event · Weekly. Admin-spawned patrol helicopter with boosted loot drops. Team participation encouraged, MVP reward for highest damage. Rewards: high-tier weapon kit, in-game currency, Event Champion Discord role.

🚢 Cargo Ship Madness

Monument PvE Event · Bi-Weekly. Timed cargo ship spawn, extra NPC difficulty, increased elite crate drops. No player raiding allowed. First squad to clear wins a bonus loot crate.

🏗️ Build Battle

Creative / Base Building · Monthly. Theme announced in Discord, limited build window, community vote for the winner (Best Fortress, Best RP Town, Most Creative Interior). Winner receives VIP perks or a special kit.

🤖 Bradley Assault

Monument PvE Challenge · Weekly. Boosted Bradley difficulty, increased crate rewards, timed competition with a fastest-takedown bonus.

🧟 Zombie Survival Night

Hardcore PvE Special. Increased NPC spawn rate, custom zombie mods, survive waves for rewards and team survival bonuses. High risk, high reward.

🎁 Event Rewards

VIP packages, priority queue access, exclusive Discord roles, rare weapons & skins, resource crates and in-game currency. We reward participation, not just winners.

⚠ Event Rules

No exploiting or glitch abuse, no griefing participants, no blocking monuments, respect staff. Admin decisions are final. Breaking event rules may result in disqualification or bans.

🤖 Final Order – Rust Automated Events

Automated events are server-triggered activities that spawn on timed or random intervals, announce globally, create high-tier loot zones and spawn custom NPC waves. You never know when the next one triggers — stay geared.

🚂 Armored Train

Mobile combat event. A train patrols the map with heavy NPC guards and loot crates onboard. Clear it and secure the crates before other players arrive.

🛰 Sputnik / Crash Site

Satellite crash with an activated radiation zone, elite NPC spawns and rare tech-based loot. Arrive fast — these attract attention.

🏪 Supermarket Takeover

Urban close-quarter combat, timed loot lock, limited access points. Perfect for aggressive squad play.

⛽ Gas Station

Quick-response event with moderate NPC difficulty. Great for solos & duos, short duration, high competition.

☄ Meteor Strike

Random world event with rare ore spawns, high-tier crates and a radiation hazard. Mine the rare materials before enemies arrive.

🚀 Missile Silo

High-tier endgame event: locked bunker, multi-wave NPC defense, elite crate unlock timer and a heavy PvP hotspot.

📅 Frequency

Small events every 30–90 min · medium every 1–3 h · large at peak times · endgame on limited daily rotation. Timers vary to prevent predictability.

🎁 Rewards

Elite weapons, explosives, rare components, custom skins, resource crates and in-game currency — all balanced to prevent pay-to-win.
