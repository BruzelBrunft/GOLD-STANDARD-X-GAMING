Who We Are

One Network.
Multiple Worlds.

GoldStandardX Gaming (GSXG) is a multi-game gaming network built around one simple mission: creating stable, community-focused gaming experiences that players and server owners can rely on.

Our network spans multiple survival and sandbox titles including Rust, ARK, DayZ, 7 Days to Die, Conan Exiles, Minecraft, Enshrouded, SCUM, and Icarus. GSXG focuses on professional server operations, active support, long-term growth, and a community-first approach.

Our goal is to create an ecosystem where communities can thrive without the burden of managing every technical and operational challenge alone. Whether you're a player looking for a great place to call home or a server owner seeking growth and support, GSXG provides the foundation to succeed.

Partner Program

Why Partner
With GSXG?

Expand Your Community

Partner communities gain access to a larger network of players across multiple games and servers. Through cross-promotion, events and collaboration, your server reaches players who may never have found you otherwise.

  • Shared player exposure across the network
  • Network-wide announcements
  • Cross-community events
  • Increased player retention
  • Expanded Discord reach

Reduce Operational Overhead

Running a successful gaming community requires more than simply hosting a server. GSXG provides the tools and support that let community leaders focus on their players rather than fighting technical issues.

  • Uptime monitoring
  • Server management assistance
  • Discord ticket systems
  • Logging and moderation tools
  • Automated server processes
  • Technical troubleshooting support
🏗️

Professional Infrastructure

GSXG has invested heavily in tools and systems designed specifically for gaming communities. Our infrastructure is built to scale alongside your community from day one.

  • Uptime Monitoring & GSXG Slave Bot
  • Discord Ticket Management
  • Server Operations Management
  • Pterodactyl Integration
  • Automated Wipe Management
  • Custom Map Hosting & Administrative Logging

Keep Your Identity

Unlike many gaming networks, GSXG does not believe every server should look the same. We provide the support and resources while letting your community remain yours.
