Privacy Policy

Gold Standard X Gaming (GSXG)  ·  Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Gold Standard X Gaming (“GSXG”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and when we may share it while operating our gaming communities, Discord servers, websites, game servers, applications, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information including:

  • Account usernames and display names
  • Discord IDs and linked gaming accounts
  • Email addresses (if provided)
  • Server activity and gameplay information
  • Support tickets and communications
  • Device, browser, and connection information
  • Purchase, subscription, VIP, and transaction records
  • Server logs, moderation records, and security data

2. How We Use Information

GSXG may use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve our services
  • Manage memberships, roles, and access permissions
  • Moderate and enforce community rules
  • Investigate abuse, cheating, fraud, or security incidents
  • Process support requests and transactions
  • Analyze performance, engagement, and service quality
  • Develop new features and community experiences

3. Sharing Information

We may share information with trusted third-party providers when necessary to operate our services, including:

  • Hosting providers
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics and monitoring platforms
  • Authentication and account linking services
  • Discord and community management tools
  • Security and fraud prevention services

Third parties may only receive information reasonably necessary to provide services to GSXG.

4. Internal Use

Information collected through GSXG services may be used internally across our communities, systems, administrative tools, moderation platforms, and affiliated GSXG operations to improve user experience, security, and service delivery.

5. Community Moderation & Enforcement

By participating in GSXG services, you acknowledge that moderation actions, reports, server logs, and enforcement records may be retained and reviewed by authorized staff.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for operational, security, legal, business, and community management purposes.

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect collected information; however, no system is completely secure.

8. Your Choices

You may request updates, corrections, or deletion of certain personal information where applicable and subject to operational or legal requirements.

9. Children’s Privacy

GSXG services are not intended for users under the minimum age required by applicable platform policies.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of GSXG services constitutes acceptance of updates.
